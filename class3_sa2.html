The Art of Picture Stories

A story isn't just a list of sentences; it is a Sequence. In Grade 3, we use digital tools to turn simple ideas into interactive experiences. Transitions bridge the gap between scenes, making the story flow smoothly.

The Storyboard Matrix

Visualizing scenes before coding them prevents bugs in the narrative flow. Each scene needs a clear beginning, action, and end.

Transition Logic

Transitions aren't just for looks—they signal a change in Time or Location to the viewer.

Information Architecture

We collect Data (facts/numbers) to make decisions. While tables are precise, Pictographs use Symbols to help us compare data at a glance.
